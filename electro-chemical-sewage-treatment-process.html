COMMERCIAL SEWAGE TREATMENT SYSTEMS
Convert commercial (industrial) wastewater to clean water for reuse.

RT PURE® Sewage Treatment system successfully eliminate the persistent pollutants from sewage using synergistic combination of electrocoagulation, electro-chemical oxidation and electro-flotation processes. System has been powered by two electrochemical reactors - PIXECELL for electro-coagulation, NURAICELL for electro-flotation and ELECTROGEN for electro-oxidation that converts the sewage water to clean and safe re-usable water.

*Commercial Sewage treatment system Skid with integrated electro-oxidation, electro-coagulation reactors, ACF, MGF Filters and Control Panel.

Certain commercial applications such as pubs, hotels, theme parks, visitors centres and industrial units needs the installation of a ‘packaged sewage treatment plant’ to receive and treat the waste water.
RT Pure Commercial sewage treatment plants are typically designed to treat the wastewater (sewage) electrochemically with the resulting ‘treated effluent’ suitable for discharge to a natural watercourse or underground drainage field / soakaway.

RT PURE® systems are modular and can be expanded to suit growing developments economically. RT PURE® Sewage Treatment Systems for Commercial Applications provides complete advanced treatment and significantly reduces BOD, TSS, the Total Nitrogen and provide better class of treated wastewater .
