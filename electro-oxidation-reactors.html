ELECTRO-OXIDATION REACTORS (SYSTEM)
Advanced Oxidation systems for wastewater treatment.

Electrogen Series STP-electro-oxidation reactors - for treatment of industrial wastewater originating from pharma industry, dairy, distillery, textile, dyeing units, meat processing industries& more. Advanced Oxidation systems Oxidates raw sewage and convert industrial/commercial/domestic waste water into clean reusable water or for safe discharge into drain.

Electrogen Oxidation Reactors helps removal of Organics, Suspended Solids, Turbidity, Algae, Fats & Oil, Grease, Heavy Metals, Odor & Colour and aids in DISINFECTION, DEODORISATION and DECOLORIZATION.

Compact, Modular Electrogen electro-oxidation reactors removes ions, FOG, Silica, Organic particulate matter using electro-oxidation process with functional De-odourization, Decolorization, BOD/COD Removal and disinfects the wastewater finally.
Electrogen Reactors can be supplied as separate units for sewage treatment which are skid-based, controlled by automatic control panel, with Optional PVC/FRP Tanks
