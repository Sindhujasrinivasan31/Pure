DOMESTIC SEWAGE TREATMENT SYSTEMS
convert residential wastewater to clean water for reuse.

RT PURE® Domestic Sewage Treatment System Models are self-contained, continual feed electrochemical systems which turn domestic waste water into clean reusable water or for safe discharge into drain.

*1 PROTECTED CONTROL PANEL *2 ELECTRO-COAGULATION REACTOR *3. ELECTRO-OXIDATION REACTOR *4 STAINLESS STEEL CABINET

Recycling domestic sewage is one way of recycling water which saves energy – treating the sewage saves money. Water conservation – using recycled wastewater to water lawns and non-food gardens conserves fresh water. Currently there is wastage, as large volumes of potable water are used on lawns and gardens.
RT PURE® Domestic sewage treatment Systems works on simple electrochemical (electrolysis) process to convert wastewater to reusable water. RT PURE® electrochemical based Domestic Wastewater treatment systems are reliable and are proven to be suitable for installation for the home or a community that wants recycling of water.
